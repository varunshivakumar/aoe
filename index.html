Alpha Omega Epsilon

A new website to attract potential recruits

Project Description: Building an interactive website for AOE (Alpha Omega Epsilon Sorority). In the past, AOE has tried to make their own websites, but their previous iterations are generic and clunky. They want a website that will really allow them to impress their peers and potential recruits.

Similar products: -

Symbiotic products: -

Objective: This website is targeted towards potential recruits of AOE. The website will showcase information about the sorority and their values as well as testimonials from sisters and benefits to joining.

Clientele: AOE (Alpha Omega Epsilon) is a professional and social sorority comprised of females majoring in engineering and technical science major. They were founded in Nov. 13th,1983 at Marquette University in Wisconsin and have been promoting friendship, leadership, and professionalism to all members of the organization, the community and their professions. The Beta-Theta chapter at Stony Brook was founded in 2011 and since AOE has been actively involved with professional and charity events on campus. Contact: sbu.aoe@gmail.com

End User: Female college students between their 1st and 3rd years who are majoring in engineering or technical science.
